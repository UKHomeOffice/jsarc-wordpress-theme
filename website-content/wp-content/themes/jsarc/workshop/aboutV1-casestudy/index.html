The Joint Security and Resilience Centre (JSaRC) is a government department bringing together government representatives, members of academia and the security sector to form a joint response to national security challenges.

Drawing on a wide pool of expertise, JSaRC works collaboratively with diverse specialists to encourage innovation and to grow opportunities in the security market.

Operating as part of the Office of Security and Counter Terrorism (OSCT), JSaRC has a central role in co-ordinating cross-government security policy.

JSaRC is the pivotal component in shaping the future of Her Majesty’s Government’s security programmes.
